The Office

Genre: Comedy, Mockumentary

First Aired: March 24, 2005

Last Aired: May 16, 2013

Seasons: 9

Episodes: 201

Creators: Greg Daniels, Ricky Gervais, Stephen Merchant


The Office is a mockumentary sitcom that depicts the everyday work lives of office employees working at Dunder Mifflin Scranton branch.

The series is known for its unique style, character development, and humor.

It has received critical acclaim and has a dedicated fan base.

The show has won numerous awards, including several Primetime Emmy Awards.
