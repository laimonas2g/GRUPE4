Sherlock

Genre: Crime, Drama, Mystery

First Aired: July 25, 2010

Last Aired: January 15, 2017

Seasons: 4

Episodes: 13

Creators: Mark Gatiss, Steven Moffat


Sherlock is a modern update to the classic detective stories of Sherlock Holmes, set in contemporary London.

The series follows Sherlock Holmes, a consulting detective, and his friend Dr. John Watson as they solve complex cases.

It is known for its clever writing, strong character development, and unique storytelling style.

The show has received critical acclaim and has a dedicated fan base.

Sherlock has won numerous awards, including several BAFTA Awards.
