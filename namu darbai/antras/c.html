Dexter

Genre: Crime, Drama, Mystery

First Aired: October 1, 2006

Last Aired: September 22, 2013

Seasons: 8

Episodes: 96

Creators: James Manos Jr., Clyde Phillips


Dexter Morgan is a blood spatter analyst for Miami Metro Police Department who leads a secret life as a vigilante serial killer.

He targets criminals who have escaped justice, using his skills to evade capture.

The series explores themes of morality, identity, and the duality of human nature.

It has received critical acclaim and has a dedicated fan base.
