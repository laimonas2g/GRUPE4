Twin Peaks

Genre: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

First Aired: April 8, 1990

Last Aired: September 3, 1991

Seasons: 2

Episodes: 30

Creators: David Lynch, Mark Frost


The body of a young girl (Laura Palmer) is washed up on a beach near the small Washington state town of Twin Peaks.

As the town's residents struggle to come to terms with the tragedy, they are drawn into a web of secrets and lies.

FBI Special Agent Dale Cooper is called in to investigate

He uncovers a complex and surreal world filled with eccentric characters, supernatural elements, and dark secrets.

The series is known for its unique blend of mystery, drama, and surrealism.
